Anime

First generation

Came in the 1910s Namakura gatana first anime to come out. Other animes in the 1910s are ōten shimokawa, jun’ichi kōuchi and seitaro kitayama. Called the fathers of anime

Second generation anime

Second generation anime started in 1923. Great kantō earthquake was one of the most famous animes during that time and also based on true events. They were competing with Disney studios at that time. A lot of the anime back then was sold out fast and out of stock. Japanese animators worked really cheaply with not that many employees. Sold abroad They competed with the quality and especially the color of anime. Made education purpose animes
